FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Show My Product
SMP is a hyperlocal delivery and product discovery platform that connects local sellers with nearby customers. We offer a reliable and affordable delivery service to help small businesses thrive while giving customers easy access to local products.
How does SMP’s delivery model work?
Our platform operates through a smart franchise-based delivery network. When a customer places an order, the local seller confirms it, and our delivery team picks it up and delivers it—usually within minutes.
How do I track my order?
You can track your order in real time through our app. From confirmation to delivery, you’ll receive live updates at every step.
